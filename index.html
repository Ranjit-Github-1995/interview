    About CCG

    CCG is a management consulting firm focused on operational efficiency and effectiveness optimization located in Fairfax, VA. Our mission is to constantly cultivate practices and processes to reduce delivery timelines with improved performance and collaboration. We have over 20 years experience serving federal, commercial, and financial clients by approaching project management using industry best practices- based in Agile and PMBOK methodologies.

    We are a small and proud group of problem solvers and change agents. Our unfair advantage is our client experience: our work centers around understanding their needs and partnering in thoughtful solutions. Our success is a result of our core values:

    • Innovation

    • Ownership

    • Teamwork

    • Diversity

    • Respect
